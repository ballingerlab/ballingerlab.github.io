Join the Lab!

We are currently recruiting postdocs, graduate students, and undergraduates who are curious and excited about organismal biology, evolution, and/or genomics!

Postdoc Candidates

If you are interested in joining the lab as a postdoctoral scholar, please email Mal with your CV and a brief description of your research experience and interests. It's also helpful if you can provide a few references (e.g., advisor or thesis committee members).

Graduate Students

If you are considering pursuing a PhD in our lab in the Department of Ecology & Evolutionary Biology, please email Mal with your CV and a brief overview of your scientific interests. Interested PhD students in other Cornell departments should also feel free to get in touch.

Undergraduate Students

We are always looking for curious and motivated undergraduates to join the lab. Email Mal with your CV/resume and a brief description of your interests and why you are interested in our lab.

We believe science works best when it's open, collaborative, fun, and driven by curiosity. Just as we value diversity in our study systems, we are committed to building a welcoming and supportive community where everyone has the opportunity to grow and succeed.
